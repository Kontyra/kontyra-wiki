Our approach to roadmaps

KONTYRA does not publish speculative timelines. The roadmap will be published when milestones are concrete enough to be meaningful — with honest status markers and no marketing inflation. A roadmap item is either In Progress, Planned, or Under Consideration. Nothing is listed as "Soon" without a real definition of soon.
